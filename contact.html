About Us

Parallel Lines Hair Studio is located in the highly desirable downtown Calgary west end at the bottom of the Vogue condominiums. We are one block away from the LRT station and ample street parking is available.

The 867 square-foot space has a sleek, zen, and open environment to showcase the talent of the service specialists while 6 individual stations allow personal attention, privacy, and a luxurious experience for each guest.
